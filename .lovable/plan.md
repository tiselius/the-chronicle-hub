

## Your Editorial Website with Sanity CMS & E-commerce

A clean, editorial-style website inspired by the mature aesthetics of publications like The New York Times—minimal, sophisticated, and content-focused.

---

### Structure Overview

**Three Pages:**
1. **Articles** — Editorial essays from Sanity CMS
2. **Store** — Physical products with Stripe checkout
3. **About** — Simple bio

---

### Design Approach

- **Typography-first design** — Serif headings (Georgia or similar) with clean sans-serif body text
- **Generous whitespace** — Breathing room between elements for a calm, readable experience  
- **Muted color palette** — Blacks, whites, and subtle grays with minimal accent colors
- **No flashy animations** — Smooth, instant interactions without distracting transitions
- **Fixed top navigation** — Simple horizontal nav with logo and page links

---

### Features

**Articles Page**
- Grid or list of editorial essays pulled from Sanity
- Article detail pages with full content, author, and publish date
- Clean reading experience with proper typography

**Store Page**
- Product grid showing items from Sanity CMS
- Product detail pages with images, descriptions, and pricing
- Shopping cart functionality
- Stripe checkout for physical products (shipping info collection)

**About Page**
- Simple bio section
- Clean, minimal layout

---

### Technical Integration

- **Sanity CMS** — Content management for both articles and products (connected to your existing project)
- **Stripe** — Full e-commerce checkout with Lovable Cloud backend
- **Lovable Cloud** — Backend for edge functions and Stripe integration

---

### Next Steps After Implementation

You'll need to:
1. Share your Sanity project ID and configure the Sanity schema for articles and products
2. Connect your Stripe account for payments
3. Add your content in Sanity CMS
